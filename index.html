Our Story

Where Every Bite Tells a Story 🫶🏻✨

Welcome to House of Taste — Wardha's beloved food destination located at the heart of Shivaji Chowk, right beside Varco Pinnacle. We're passionate about serving fresh, flavorful, and beautifully crafted food that brings people together.

From our signature Nawabzade Sandwiches to hand-crafted pizzas, creamy lasagna, and sizzling Chinese — every dish is made with love, fresh ingredients, and a commitment to quality. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a cozy hangout with friends, we've got you covered.
